Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how YaarWin collects, uses and protects information about people who use the yaarwin gaming application and this website. The policy is written in plain language because we believe transparency is part of trust.

By using the platform you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the application.

Who This Policy Applies To

Players, visitors to this website and anyone who interacts with the platform through official channels.

Changes to This Policy

We may update the policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the website or the application.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the gaming application, keep accounts secure and improve the experience.

Account Information

Identifiers required to create and maintain your account.

Usage Information

Aggregated and individual usage signals that help us understand how the application is used.

Device Information

Basic device information used for compatibility, performance and security.

Communication Information

Messages you send to us through support and feedback channels.

How We Use Information

Information is used to operate the application, support players, protect accounts, improve the product and communicate important updates.

Operating the Platform

Authentication, matchmaking, community features and other core experiences.

Improving the Experience

Understanding usage patterns to make better design decisions.

Security and Safety

Detecting and responding to abuse, fraud and other harmful behaviour.

Communication

Sending important updates such as policy changes and major announcements.

Cookies, Analytics and Third Parties

This website uses a small number of cookies and analytics signals to understand how visitors use the site and to make it work properly. Where appropriate, third-party services help us deliver specific features. We choose partners that share our commitment to player trust.

Cookies

Used for essential functionality and lightweight analytics.

Analytics

Aggregated metrics that help us improve the website and the application.

Third Parties

Service providers are bound by appropriate terms and are not permitted to use information for purposes outside of our service.

User Rights, Data Security and Information Usage

You have rights regarding your information, including access, correction and deletion where applicable. We protect information with appropriate technical and organisational measures.

Your Rights

Contact the team through the Contact page to exercise your rights.

Security

We protect information using modern security practices and continue to invest in improvements.

Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described.

Privacy Commitments

We commit to handling information responsibly, communicating clearly and giving players reasonable control over their data. For questions about this policy, contact us through the contact page.

Our Promise

We will continue to write our policies in plain language and explain changes in advance when possible.
